Exotic dance moves

As part of my post-doctorate work,I did study exotic tribal dances.My favourite dances are as follows:
Polynesian chicken dance
Dating back as far as 300BC,a whole village would dance around in a circle like
chickens,to encourage their livestock.
Icelandic brownian shuffle
Before the Icelanders developed fire as a means of getting warm,they used to practice this dance
which involved hudding close together in a circle on the floor,and shuffling their bodies around
in imperceptibly tiny,very rapid movements.
Arctic robot dance
An interesting example of historic misinformation,English explorers in the 1960s believed to have
discovered a new dance style characterised by "robotic",stilted movements,being practiced by
inhabitants of Northern Alaska and Canada.
For more of my research,see my exotic dance research page.
